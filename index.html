PolyAxis Alignment Quiz

Welcome to the PolyAxis political alignment quiz. It's intended to assess one's political leanings along Left/Right, Authoritarian/Libertarian, Pragmatist/Idealist, and Populist/Elitist axes. I know there are a lot of answer choices, but I really wanted to make sure they captured more of the nuance that exists when people (or at least I) approach questions like these. Normally there is one neutral option, but I wanted to distinguish between a 'true neutral' and someone who both agrees as well aside disagrees with the statement. Choosing the 'unsure' option will mean your answer to the question is not counted for determining alignment along that specific axis.
